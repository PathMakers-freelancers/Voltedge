Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our infrastructure solutions

What is the typical implementation timeline?

Implementation timelines vary based on scale. Small deployments (under 100 nodes) typically take 4-6 weeks. Enterprise deployments can range from 3-6 months with phased rollouts to ensure zero downtime.

Do you support legacy infrastructure integration?

Yes, VoltEdge is designed to integrate with legacy systems through our comprehensive adapter library. We support SCADA, DCS, and most proprietary protocols used in the energy sector.

What happens if there's a system failure?

Our systems are designed with N+2 redundancy. In the unlikely event of a failure, automatic failover occurs within milliseconds. Our 24/7 NOC is immediately alerted and begins remediation.

Can VoltEdge be deployed on-premise?

Absolutely. We offer cloud, hybrid, and fully on-premise deployment options. Many of our critical infrastructure clients prefer on-premise installations for security and compliance reasons.

What security certifications do you hold?

VoltEdge holds ISO 27001, SOC 2 Type II, and NERC CIP certifications. We undergo annual third-party penetration testing and maintain compliance with GDPR, HIPAA, and other regional regulations.

How does AI-driven predictive maintenance work?

Our ML models analyze thousands of data points including vibration, temperature, voltage fluctuations, and historical patterns to predict equipment failures 2-4 weeks in advance with 94% accuracy.
