Service FAQs

What is your typical response time for non-emergencies? +

For standard service calls, we typically schedule within 24-48 hours. However, Platinum members get same-day priority scheduling for all requests.

Do you provide free estimates? +

Yes! For all new installation projects, we provide a complimentary on-site assessment and detailed quote. Diagnostic visits for repairs may incur a small trip fee which is waived if work is performed.

Are you licensed and insured? +

Absolutely. VoltEdge holds Master Electrician licenses in all 15 states we operate in and carries $5M in liability insurance for commercial projects.

Can you help with LEED certification? +

Yes, our engineers are LEED accredited and can design electrical systems that contribute significant points towards your building's Green certification.
